Grilled Cheese Sandwich recipe!

Learn how to make a delicious Grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Cheeder cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Put your pan on medium heat.
  2. Spread your butter on two slices of bread.
  3. Place your buttered bread on the pan.
  4. Wait 3 Mins for the bread to toast.
  5. Then place your cheese on the bread and wait for it to melt.
  6. When finished cooking put it together.
